About Agra Fort

Agra Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It was the main residence of the Mughal emperors before the capital shifted to Delhi.

History

The fort was originally built by Emperor Akbar in 1565. Later emperors like Jahangir and Shah Jahan added beautiful palaces and mosques inside the fort.

Architecture

Built mainly of red sandstone, the fort features massive walls, royal halls, Diwan-i-Aam, Diwan-i-Khas, and beautiful marble structures.

Major Attractions Inside

• Jahangir Mahal • Khas Mahal • Musamman Burj • Diwan-i-Aam

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time due to pleasant weather.

Entry Fee & Timings

Indian Visitors: ₹50 Foreign Visitors: ₹650 Timings: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM
