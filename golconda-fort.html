Golconda Fort, Hyderabad

Back to Home

Golconda Fort, built in 1143, is famous for diamond trade and acoustic wonders.

History

Capital of the Qutb Shahi dynasty, it was a center for diamond mining and trade.

Architecture

Granite walls, palaces, and the famous acoustic dome. Offers panoramic views.

How to Reach

Location: Hyderabad outskirts. Ticket: INR 15. Best time: October-February.
