Amber Fort, Jaipur

Back to Home

Amber Fort, also known as Amer Fort, is a majestic palace located in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Built in 1592 by Raja Man Singh, it showcases a beautiful blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture.

History

Constructed during the reign of Mughal Emperor Akbar, it served as the capital of the Kachwaha Rajputs. The fort has witnessed many royal ceremonies and historic events.

Architecture

The fort features red sandstone and marble construction, intricate carvings, Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace), Sukh Niwas and Ganesh Pol.

How to Reach

Location: 11 km from Jaipur
Ticket: ₹200 (Indian)
Best Time to Visit: October – March
