About Hampi Palaces

Hampi Palaces are part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Karnataka. They were once the royal center of the powerful Vijayanagara Empire.

History

Hampi was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th–16th century. It was one of the richest cities in the world during its peak before being destroyed in 1565.

Architecture

The palaces showcase Indo-Islamic architecture with royal enclosures, lotus mahal, elephant stables, and massive temples.

Famous Structures

• Lotus Mahal • Elephant Stables • Royal Enclosure • Hazara Rama Temple

Best Time to Visit

October to February is ideal due to pleasant weather.
