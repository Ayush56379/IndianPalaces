Hampi Palaces, Karnataka

Back to Home

Hampi is a UNESCO site with Vijayanagara ruins, including palaces and stone chariots.

History

Capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, destroyed in 1565. It has temples and palaces.

Architecture

Stone structures, chariots, and Lotus Mahal. Known for Dravidian style.

How to Reach

Location: Hospet, Karnataka. Ticket: INR 30. Best time: October-February.
