Nahargarh Fort, Jaipur

Back to Home

Nahargarh Fort, built in 1734, is a hill fort with palaces and offers sunset views of Jaipur.

History

Used as a retreat for royals, it has rooms for queens and a temple.

Architecture

Sandstone walls, Madhavendra Palace, and frescoes. Known for its acoustics and gardens.

How to Reach

Location: Jaipur outskirts. Ticket: INR 50. Best time: October-March.
