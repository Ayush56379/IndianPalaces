Jaigarh Fort, Jaipur

Back to Home

Jaigarh Fort, built in 1726, overlooks Amber Fort and is famous for the world's largest cannon, Jaivana.

History

Constructed for defense, it was never conquered. The fort has underground passages and water systems.

Architecture

Red sandstone walls, palaces, and the cannon museum. Offers views of Jaipur and Maota Lake.

How to Reach

Location: Near Amber Fort. Ticket: INR 35. Best time: October-March.
