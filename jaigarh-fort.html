About Jaigarh Fort

Jaigarh Fort is a magnificent hilltop fort located near Amber Fort in Jaipur, Rajasthan. It is known for its strong military structure and stunning views of the Aravalli hills.

History

The fort was built in 1726 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II to protect Amber Fort. It served as a strong defense fort and housed important artillery.

Famous Attraction – Jaivana Cannon

Jaigarh Fort is home to the world’s largest cannon on wheels, known as the Jaivana Cannon. It was manufactured inside the fort and remains one of its main attractions.

Architecture

The fort features massive walls, watchtowers, underground water tanks, and military storage rooms. It reflects the strength and strategic planning of Rajput architecture.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is between October and March when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Entry Fee & Timings

Indian Visitors: ₹100 Foreign Visitors: ₹200 Timings: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Open daily)
