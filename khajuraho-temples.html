Khajuraho Temples, built in the 10th century, are UNESCO sites with erotic sculptures.

History

Chandela dynasty temples, known for tantric art.

Architecture

Sandstone carvings, shikharas, and detailed sculptures.

How to Reach

Location: Khajuraho town. Ticket: INR 40. Best time: October-March.
