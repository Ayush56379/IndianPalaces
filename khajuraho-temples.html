About Khajuraho Temples

Khajuraho Temples are a group of ancient Hindu and Jain temples located in Madhya Pradesh. They are famous worldwide for their detailed sculptures and artistic architecture.

History

The temples were built between 950 and 1050 AD by the Chandela dynasty. Originally, there were around 85 temples, but today only about 25 remain.

Architecture

The temples are known for Nagara-style architecture and beautifully carved stone sculptures depicting gods, daily life, mythology, and cultural traditions.

Major Attractions

• Kandariya Mahadev Temple • Lakshmana Temple • Vishvanatha Temple • Western Group of Temples

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit due to pleasant weather conditions.

Entry Fee & Timings
