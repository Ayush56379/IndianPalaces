Orchha Fort

Bundela Palace Complex of Madhya Pradesh

History

Built in 1531 by the Bundela Rajput ruler Rudra Pratap Singh, Orchha Fort served as the capital of the Bundela kingdom. It includes Jahangir Mahal, Raj Mahal and several temples.

Architecture

The fort features sandstone palaces, chhatris (cenotaphs), intricate murals and scenic river views. Jahangir Mahal is the most famous structure inside the complex.
