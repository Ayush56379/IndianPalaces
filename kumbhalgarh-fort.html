Kumbhalgarh Fort, Rajsamand

Back to Home

Kumbhalgarh Fort, built in 1443, is the second largest fort with temples and wildlife sanctuary.

History

Founded by Rana Kumbha, it has never been conquered. It has 365 temples and palaces.

Architecture

Long walls, palaces, and temples. Known for its impregnable design and scenic views.

How to Reach

Location: Rajsamand district. Ticket: INR 15. Best time: October-March.
