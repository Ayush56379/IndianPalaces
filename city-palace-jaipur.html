City Palace, Jaipur

Back to Home

City Palace is the royal residence of the Maharaja of Jaipur, built in 1727. It blends Rajput and Mughal styles and houses museums.

History

Constructed by Sawai Jai Singh II, it has been the seat of the Kachwaha dynasty.

Architecture

Features pink sandstone, intricate carvings, and halls like Diwan-e-Khas. The palace has a museum with royal artifacts.

How to Reach

Location: Jaipur city center. Ticket: INR 200. Best time: October-March.
