Kangra Fort, Himachal Pradesh

Back to Home

Kangra Fort, built in the 4th century, is an ancient fort with temples and mountain views.

History

Oldest fort in India, ruled by various dynasties.

Architecture

Stone walls, temples, and palaces. Scenic location.

How to Reach

Location: Kangra town. Ticket: INR 20. Best time: April-June, September-November.
