Humayun's Tomb

Magnificent Mughal Mausoleum in Delhi

History

Humayun's Tomb was built in 1570 by Empress Bega Begum in memory of Emperor Humayun. It is one of the finest examples of early Mughal architecture in India and inspired the design of the Taj Mahal.

Architecture

The monument is built with red sandstone and white marble. It features Persian-style gardens, grand arches and a majestic dome.
