Humayun's Tomb, Delhi

Back to Home

Humayun's Tomb, built in 1570, is the first Mughal mausoleum with gardens and domes.

History

Commissioned by Humayun's wife, it inspired Taj Mahal. It has other tombs in the complex.

Architecture

Red sandstone and marble, Persian-style gardens, and symmetrical design.

How to Reach

Location: Nizamuddin, Delhi. Ticket: INR 30. Best time: October-March.
