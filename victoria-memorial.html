About Victoria Memorial

Victoria Memorial is a grand marble monument located in Kolkata, West Bengal. Built between 1906 and 1921, it was dedicated to Queen Victoria and now serves as a museum.

History

After the death of Queen Victoria in 1901, Lord Curzon proposed the idea of building a grand memorial. It stands today as a symbol of British-era architecture in India.

Architecture

The monument is built using white Makrana marble and blends British and Mughal architectural styles.

Major Attractions

• Grand Marble Structure • Museum Galleries • Royal Paintings • Beautiful Gardens

Best Time to Visit

October to February is the best time due to pleasant weather.
