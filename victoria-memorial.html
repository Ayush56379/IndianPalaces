Victoria Memorial, Kolkata

Back to Home

Victoria Memorial, built in 1921, is a marble palace with British history exhibits.

History

Dedicated to Queen Victoria, it has art galleries and royal artifacts.

Architecture

White marble, domes, and gardens. Indo-Saracenic style.

How to Reach

Location: Kolkata city center. Ticket: INR 30. Best time: October-February.
