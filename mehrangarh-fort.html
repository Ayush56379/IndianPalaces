Mehrangarh Fort, Jodhpur

One of India's Largest and Most Magnificent Forts

History

Mehrangarh Fort was built in 1459 by Rao Jodha. It served as the stronghold of the Rathore dynasty and stands proudly on a rocky hill overlooking Jodhpur.

Architecture

The fort features massive sandstone walls, grand courtyards, intricately decorated palaces like Moti Mahal, and a museum displaying royal artifacts, weapons and paintings.
