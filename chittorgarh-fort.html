Chittorgarh Fort is India's largest fort, built in the 7th century, known for its grand towers and historic palaces.

History

The fort was the site of heroic battles, including the famous jauhar of Rani Padmini. It contains Jain temples and royal cenotaphs.

Architecture

Massive stone walls, Kirti Stambh, Vijay Stambh, and historic palaces like Rana Kumbha Palace define its architectural beauty.
